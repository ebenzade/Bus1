Jos Metro Bus Tracker:
This repository is about a web app solution to achieve Digital Inclusion with an Assistive technology in the area of providing a bus route tracing app.
It seeks to solve the problem of not knowing the current location of a bus on a particular bus route, thereby assisting commuters to plan their movement by bus and be at the stops in good time to board the bus.
(NB: AI assistance used in the process; Copilot and Loveable).
